City Lines

These are four maps of different cities around the world.
Top Left is Tokyo
Top Right is Paris
Bottom Left is Shanghai
and the Bottom Right is New York
Each have their subway lines shown to compare the density, structure length, and creation years. Click on the lines to find out about their information.

The basemap from Mapbox is created entirely of lines. By only displaying lines of cities, it gives you an better idea of block sizes and the density of the street scape. For instance, comparing at the same zoom level, New York has an extremely organized grid system compared to the others, Tokyo seems to have more streets overall with somewhat organized grids, and Shanghai has a lot of white space - may be structures or open space -.
There are three zoom levels you can choose to view the map, click on the lines to show popups.

Basemap created on Mapbox.
Subway lines were taken from Open Street Map, Nextzen Metro Extracts Shapefile.
